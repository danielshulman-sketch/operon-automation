# User Guide

This guide helps regular users connect email, train voice, use AI drafts, and manage automations.

## 1) Getting Started
- Log in with your account.
- Use the sidebar to access Email Stream, AI Chat, and Automations.

## 2) Email Stream
### Connect email
- Go to Email Stream.
- Click "Connect Email".
- Choose OAuth (Gmail) or SMTP/IMAP.
- Follow prompts and save.

### Auto sync
- Go to Settings -> Automations.
- Enable "Auto Sync Emails".
- Choose an interval.

### Delete emails
- Open an email and use the Delete action.

## 3) Voice Training
- Go to Settings.
- Click "Train Voice".
- Add sample emails and answer the questions.
- Save to build your voice profile.

## 4) AI Drafts
- Open an email and click "Generate Draft".
- Drafts are created in your voice profile style.
- You can edit drafts before sending.

### Auto draft
- Go to Settings -> Automations.
- Enable "Auto Drafts".
- Choose which categories should auto-generate drafts.

## 5) AI Chat
- Go to AI Chat.
- Choose "Persona AI" to answer in your voice.
- Use "Business" chat for knowledge base answers.

## 6) Integrations
- Go to Automations -> Manage Integrations.
- Connect tools required by your automations.
- If OAuth is required and you see "Missing OAuth Config", ask your admin to add provider keys.

## 7) Automations
### Create automation
- Go to Automations -> Create Automation.
- Pick a trigger and add steps.
- Save and enable.

### View runs
- Open an automation to see recent runs and status.

## 8) Troubleshooting
- "Missing OAuth config": ask admin to configure provider keys.
- "Failed to generate draft": ensure AI keys are configured.
- "Emails not syncing": verify email credentials and sync settings.
